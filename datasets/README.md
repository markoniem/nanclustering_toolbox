#### Sim5D2 and Sim2D2 data sets ####  
**Description:**    
Synthetic two-dimensional data with Gaussian clusters and different degree 
of cluster overlap and noise. Sim2D2 consists of two clusters (2000 observations) and Sim5D2 consists of five clusters (2970 observations).    
**Reference:**    
S. Jauhiainen and T. Kärkkäinen, "A simple cluster validation index with maximal coverage" *In Proceedings of the 25th European Symposium on Artificial Neural Networks, Computational Intelligence, and Machine Learning,* pages 293-298, ESANN, 2017.

#### O200 and O2000 data sets ####  
**Description:**     
Synthetic two-dimensional data with Gaussian or Laplacian distributed clusters (in total K=5 clusters) with different degrees of cluster overlap and noise.   
**Reference:**   
M. Niemelä, S. Äyrämö, and T. Kärkkäinen, "Comparison of cluster validation indices with missing data" *In Proceedings of the 26th European Symposium on Artificial Neural Networks, Computational Intelligence, and Machine Learning,* pages 461-466, ESANN, 2018.

#### csai ####  
**Description:**    
Data sets from a previous study, ordered as follows: S1, S2, S3, S4, Sim5D2, Sim2D2, O200, O2000. Data sets consists of various degrees of missing values (0%, 5%, 10%, and 20%).     
**Reference:**    
M. Niemelä and T. Kärkkäinen, "Improving clustering and cluster validation with 
  missing data using distance estimation methods," *In T. Tuovinen, J. Periaux, and 
  P. Neittaanmäki, editors, Computational Sciences and Artificial Intelligence in

#### M-Spheres data sets ####      
**Description:**    
Synthetic multidimensional data sets with varied dimensions (M=10-100) and distances between cluster centroids (dc=0.6-0.9).  
**Data sets generator:**    
https://github.com/jookriha/M_Spheres_Dataset_Generator  
**Reference:**    
J. Hämäläinen, T. Kärkkäinen, and T. Rossi, "Improving scalable k-means++", *Algorithms, 14(1),* 2021.
